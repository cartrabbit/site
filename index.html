Looking To Join Our Team?

We have offices in Singapore and Coimbatore, India. We offer one of the very modern workspaces. When we say modern we primarily refer to our work culture (Not the tall buildings, for example). You will experience complete freedom and enjoy working, rather than struggling with it. We do not have hierarchies and project managers.

There are no meetings (Yes. We believe that the meetings are a boring way of killing time.). We instead prefer to have short discussions that are meaningful and help taking quick decisions.

Finished your tasks for the day? Get out and enjoy your life!
